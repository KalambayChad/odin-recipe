Description

Assemble this fun, portable dish right inside a bag of chips. Reviewers report that their kids love eating this for dinner. "This is such an easy and quick meal that the whole family is sure to enjoy!" —reviewer mommyoftwins

ingredient
  • 3/4cup water
  • 1(1 ounce) package tacos seasoning mix
  • 4(2.5 ounce) packages corn chips
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 chopped fresh tomato
  • 1/2 cup our cream
  • 1/3 cup salsa
step
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain excess oil. Mix in water and taco seasoning. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Gently crush corn chips in the sealed bags. Snip one top and one bottom corner off each bag, then cut open along the side edge.
  4. Spoon equal amounts of beef, lettuce, Cheddar, tomato, sour cream, and salsa into each bag on top of crushed chips.
  5. Serve in the bag and eat using a fork. Enjoy!
